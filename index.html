A Practical Workflow

1

Define Learner Needs

↓

Use frameworks to define learning objectives. Specify the learner profile, target skill, and proficiency level.

Key Tool: GSE Teacher Toolkit to align materials with GSE/CEFR standards.

2

Choose Your AI Co-Pilot

↓

Select a powerful Generative AI model and assign a specific role for your pedagogical goal.

  • ChatGPT: The Content Creator for lessons, dialogues, and scripts.
  • Gemini: The App Developer, using the Canvas feature to build interactive apps.
  • Copilot: The Practice Partner for speaking scenarios.
3

Generate Diverse Media

↓

Go beyond text. Create engaging, multimodal content to cater to different learning styles.

Key Platform: Google AI Studio

Images Speech Video Music
4

Use Specialized ELT Tools

↓

Leverage platforms designed specifically for education to enhance your teaching workflow.

Platform Example: NoteLab English, a dedicated teacher tool from Trinity College.
